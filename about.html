Who am I?

I'm Bahman, 42 years old, and a newly settled "dalabo".

My girlfriend Katarina, our two-year-old son Otis, and I recently made the move from Stockholm to Falun. We found our dream home here, fulfilling our wish of giving Otis a backyard to play in and his very own swing.

Hobbies

I'm a real movie-buff and video game-nerd. I'm also a collector, I've managed to acquire thousands of video games and movies throughout the years.

Don’t ask me what my favorite game or movie is, though—I have far too many to choose from, and my memory isn’t what it used to be.


Additionally, I have a passion for digital design and photography, even though it's been years since I last took a photo or explored my creative side through design. I'm hoping to rekindle that spark and start capturing moments again.

Future goals

Leaving Stockholm sadly meant saying goodbye to my role as an IT coordinator. Since relocating to Dalarna, I've not found any job opportunities in my previous field.

To adapt and grow, I've just recently enrolled in a few courses focused on web development (such as this one), aiming to transition into a new career as a web developer. I have some limited experience in this area from about 17 years ago, but unfortunately this is more or less a completely fresh start, as so much has changed. HTML 5 and responsive web design wasn't even a thing back then!


So I'm hoping to learn HTML 5 and CSS 3 so I can, somewhat, catch up in this ever-evolving field.
